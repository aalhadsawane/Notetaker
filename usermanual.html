NoteMaker.

Make a note!

Give your note a title. (not nescessary)
Give a description. (not nescessary either)
Note: You must fill in either title or description or else no note will be made.

Search through your notes!

Just start entering your note title in the searchbar.

Maintain your notes!

As you make a note it will have the month name and date on it. The most recent ones will be on the top!!

Edit notes!

Edit notes just by clicking on the edit icon on the note

View more efficiently!

If you click on the note without being in edit mode, the note's content wraps up and now you see the heading. This really helps in keeping things organised and not ending up being messy and confused out.

Delete notes!

Edit notes just by clicking on the delete icon on the note
